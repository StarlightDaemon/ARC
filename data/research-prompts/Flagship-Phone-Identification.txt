# Flagship Phone Lineup Research - Brand Priority Identification

## OBJECTIVE

Identify current FLAGSHIP/PREMIUM phones for each major Android brand that warrant accessory guides. Focus on mainline/premium series only (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S, NOT Galaxy A).

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

For each brand, provide:

```
## [BRAND NAME]

**Flagship Series:** [Series name, e.g., "Galaxy S", "Pixel"]

**Current Flagship Models (2024-2025):**

1. **[Phone Model Name]** - [Release Date]
   - Screen Size: X.X"
   - Special Features: [S Pen, Foldable, Alert Slider, etc.]
   - Why Include: [Flagship status, unique features, high XDA interest]

2. **[Phone Model Name]** - [Release Date]
   - Screen Size: X.X"
   - Special Features: [Features]
   - Why Include: [Reason]

**Exclude (Budget/Mid-range lines):**
- [Series to skip, e.g., "Galaxy A series", "Pixel a series"]

---
```

---

## BRANDS TO RESEARCH

### 1. Samsung
- **Focus on:** Galaxy S series, Galaxy Z Fold/Flip
- **Exclude:** Galaxy A series, Galaxy M series, Galaxy F series
- **Special note:** Include both S Ultra AND regular S if both exist
- **Foldables:** Include Z Fold and Z Flip flagships

### 2. Google  
- **Focus on:** Pixel mainline, Pixel Pro, Pixel Fold
- **Exclude:** Pixel "a" series (budget line)
- **Special note:** Include both Pro and Pro XL if sizing differs

### 3. OnePlus
- **Focus on:** Numbered series (OnePlus X), OnePlus Open (foldable)
- **Exclude:** OnePlus Nord series, OnePlus Ace series
- **Special note:** Alert Slider is OnePlus signature feature

### 4. Nothing
- **Focus on:** Phone numbered series (Phone 1, 2, 3)
- **Exclude:** Phone (a) series if it exists
- **Special note:** Glyph Interface is signature feature

### 5. Motorola
- **Focus on:** Edge series (Edge+, Edge Ultra)
- **Exclude:** Moto G series, Moto E series
- **Special note:** Look for TurboPower charging specs

### 6. Sony (if applicable)
- **Focus on:** Xperia 1 series, Xperia 5 series
- **Exclude:** Xperia 10 series
- **Special note:** 21:9 displays, dedicated camera button

### 7. ASUS (if applicable)
- **Focus on:** ROG Phone series (gaming flagships), Zenfone series
- **Exclude:** Mid-range Zenfone variants

---

## SELECTION CRITERIA

Include phones that meet **at least 3** of these:

✓ **Flagship processor** (Snapdragon 8 Gen X, Google Tensor, Dimensity 9xxx, etc.)  
✓ **Flagship hardware** (Premium build, advanced cameras, high refresh displays)  
✓ **Unique features** (S Pen, Alert Slider, Glyph, Foldable, etc.)  
✓ **Active XDA community** (high forum activity, developer support)  
✓ **Wide US availability** (sold unlocked on Amazon/Best Buy/Carrier stores)  
✓ **Mainline series** (Not budget/mid-range variants)

---

## TIMEFRAME

Focus on phones released:
- **2025:** Current year flagships (highest priority)
- **2024:** Recent flagships (high priority)
- **2023:** Include if flagship-tier hardware (still relevant)
- **Pre-2023:** Exclude (accessory market has moved on)
