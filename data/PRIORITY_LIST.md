## PRIORITY TIER 1 (High Community Interest)

### 1. Google Pixel 10 Pro XL (Aug 2025)
- **Why:** Native Qi2 magnets, Tensor G5, reference Android device
- **Unique features:** Pixelsnap magnetic, ultrasonic fingerprint, 37W PPS charging
- **Accessory challenges:** Flat display (easy protectors), native magnets (no case required)
- **Research status:** Has detailed Gemini analysis already
- **Recommended for:** Perplexity Pro comparison test ⭐

### 2. Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra (Feb 2025)
- **Why:** S Pen accessories, market leader, case-dependent Qi2
- **Unique features:** S Pen silo, titanium frame, no native magnets
- **Accessory challenges:** Magnetic cases required for wireless, S Pen interference
- **Research status:** Has detailed Gemini analysis

### 3. Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 7 (July 2025)
- **Why:** Foldable screen protector needs, S Pen support, 8.0" tablet mode
- **Unique features:** Film-only inner screen, 4.2mm thin
- **Accessory challenges:** Special film protectors, hinge protection
- **Research status:** Has detailed Gemini analysis

---

## PRIORITY TIER 2 (Strong Demand)

### 4. Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold (Oct 2025)
- **Why:** Foldable + native Qi2, IP68 rating
- **Unique features:** First IP68 foldable with magnets
- **Accessory challenges:** Film protectors for inner screen, magnetic case compatibility

### 5. Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 7 (July 2025)
- **Why:** Compact foldable, FlexWindow accessories
- **Unique features:** Vapor chamber cooling, clamshell form factor
- **Accessory challenges:** Unique shape, hinge stress points

### 6. Nothing Phone (3) (July 2025)
- **Why:** Glyph Interface accessories, design-focused community
- **Unique features:** Unique aesthetic, sub-$800 flagship
- **Accessory challenges:** Standard Qi (no Qi2), transparent cases for Glyph

---

## PRIORITY TIER 3 (Niche/Specialist)

### 7. Samsung Galaxy S25 Edge (May 2025)
- **Why:** Ultra-slim 5.8mm design challenges case makers
- **Unique features:** Thinnest flagship, 200MP camera
- **Accessory challenges:** Minimal bulk cases, thermal management

### 8. ASUS ROG Phone 9 (Nov 2024)
- **Why:** Gaming accessories ecosystem, active cooling
- **Unique features:** AirTriggers, 185Hz display, AeroActive Cooler
- **Accessory challenges:** Gaming-specific mounts, cooling attachments

### 9. Motorola Razr Ultra 2025 (Apr 2025)
- **Why:** Large flip phone, 7.0" inner display
- **Unique features:** 165Hz screen, teardrop hinge
- **Accessory challenges:** Clamshell shape, large cover screen

---

## PRIORITY TIER 4 (Lower Volume)

### 10. Motorola Edge 60 Ultra (Apr 2025)
- **Why:** 125W fast charging ecosystem
- **Unique features:** Ultra-fast wired charging
- **Accessory challenges:** Standard flagship accessories

### 11. Sony Xperia 1 VII (June 2025)
- **Why:** Pro camera accessories, legacy ports (3.5mm jack)
- **Unique features:** Optical zoom, microSD, manual controls
- **Accessory challenges:** Niche market, standard accessories

### 12. ASUS Zenfone 12 Ultra (Feb 2025)
- **Why:** Gimbal stabilizer, video creator accessories
- **Unique features:** 6-Axis hybrid gimbal
- **Accessory challenges:** Standard flagship accessories

### 13. Google Pixel 9 Pro XL (Aug 2024)
- **Why:** Bridge device, still widely sold
- **Unique features:** Tensor G4, lower price than Pixel 10
- **Accessory challenges:** No native Qi2, standard accessories
