```

- Brand name
- Case name
- MagSafe/Qi2/Pixelsnap compatible? (Yes/No)
- Port cover? (Yes/No)
- Camera cover? (Yes/No)
- Kickstand? (Yes/No)
- Direct purchase URL

**SCREEN PROTECTORS:**
- Tempered Glass options (ZAGG Glass XTR4, Spigen GLAS.tR, Whitestone Dome, dbrand Prism, OtterBox, amFilm, Ringke)
- Film/Polymer options (Spigen Neo Flex, Ringke Dual Easy, ArmorSuit)

For EACH protector, provide:
- Brand, Product name, Camera notch style (Hole Punch/Covered/Cut Out), Material, Warranty, Purchase URL

**CHARGERS:**
- Wired chargers with wattage (Google 45W/67W, Anker, UGREEN, Belkin, Spigen)
- Wireless/Qi2 chargers (Pixel Stand, Belkin, Anker MagGo)
```
