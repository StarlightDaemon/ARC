# Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold Accessories Research

## Device: Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold

### Critical Device Information:
- **Release Date**: August 2025
- **Form Factor**: Book-style foldable
- **Key Features**:
  - Tensor G5 (TSMC 3nm) processor
  - 8.0" Main Display (inner, foldable OLED)
  - 6.3" Cover Display (outer, flat OLED)
  - Native Qi2 magnets (Pixelsnap) - BUILT INTO DEVICE
  - Ultrasonic fingerprint sensor
  - Triple rear camera system
  - Android 16

### Charging Specifications:
- **Wired**: 45W USB-PD PPS
- **Wireless**: 25W Qi2 Pixelsnap (magnetic)

---

## Research Task

Research and compile a comprehensive list of **phone cases** for the Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold.

### Requirements:

**CRITICAL - Fold-Specific Design:**
- Cases MUST be designed for foldable phones
- Should NOT interfere with folding mechanism
- Protect both hinge and outer surfaces
- Cases for regular phones will NOT fit

**CRITICAL - Pixelsnap/Magnetic Compatibility:**
- The Pixel 10 Pro Fold has BUILT-IN magnets (Qi2/Pixelsnap)
- Prioritize cases that:
  - Are designed FOR Pixelsnap/Qi2 (magnetic cases)
  - Explicitly state Pixelsnap/MagSafe/Qi2 compatibility
  - Include "magnetic" or "MagFit" in product name/description

**Categories to Find (Priority Order):**
1. **Official Google Cases** - Pixelsnap-compatible cases from Google Store
2. **Fold-Specific Rugged Cases** - Heavy-duty protection (OtterBox, UAG, etc.)
3. **Premium Fold Cases** - High-quality materials (leather, aramid fiber)
4. **Hinge Protection Cases** - Specific hinge/spine protection
5. **Slim Fold Cases** - Minimal bulk while protecting hinge
6. **Eco-Friendly Cases** - Sustainable materials

**For Each Product, Provide:**
- Brand name
- Product name (exact, from official listing)
- Price (USD, from official/Amazon listing)
- Material (e.g., "TPU + PC", "Silicone", "Leather")
- Key features (hinge protection, magnetic compatibility, kickstand, drop rating)
- Purchase links (official store, Amazon, Best Buy, etc.)
- Availability status (in stock / pre-order / out of stock)

**Important Notes:**
- Focus on cases SPECIFICALLY for Pixel 10 Pro Fold (foldable form factor)
- Note if case protects hinge/spine area
- Verify products are actually available for purchase
- Include both well-known brands AND lesser-known quality options
- For magnetic cases, note if they work with Pixelsnap accessories
- Include drop protection ratings when available (MIL-STD-810G, etc.)
- Note if case allows folding to lay flat

**Output Format:**
Provide results as a markdown table or structured list that can be easily converted to JSON.
