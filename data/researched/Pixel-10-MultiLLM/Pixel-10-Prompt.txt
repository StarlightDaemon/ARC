# Google Pixel 10 Accessories Research

## Device: Google Pixel 10

### Critical Device Information:
- **Release Date**: August 2025
- **Key Features**:
  - Tensor G5 (TSMC 3nm) processor
  - 6.3" Super Actua Display (flat design, smaller than Pro)
  - Native Qi2 magnets (Pixelsnap) - BUILT INTO DEVICE
  - Ultrasonic fingerprint sensor
  - Dual rear camera system
  - Android 16

### Charging Specifications:
- **Wired**: 45W USB-PD PPS
- **Wireless**: 25W Qi2 Pixelsnap (magnetic)

## Research Task

Research and compile a comprehensive list of **phone cases** for the Google Pixel 10 (base model).

### Requirements:

**CRITICAL - Pixelsnap/Magnetic Compatibility:**
- The Pixel 10 has BUILT-IN magnets (Qi2/Pixelsnap)
- Prioritize cases that:
  - Are designed FOR Pixelsnap/Qi2 (magnetic cases)
  - Explicitly state Pixelsnap/MagSafe/Qi2 compatibility
  - Include "magnetic" or "MagFit" in product name/description

**Categories to Find (Priority Order):**
1. **Official Google Cases** - Pixelsnap-compatible cases from Google Store
2. **Rugged Cases** - Heavy-duty protection (OtterBox, Poetic, SUPCASE, UAG, etc.)
3. **Premium Cases** - High-quality materials (leather, aramid fiber, etc.)
4. **Everyday/Hybrid Cases** - Balance of protection and style (Spigen, Caseology, etc.)
5. **Clear Cases** - Transparent/showcase design
6. **Slim Cases** - Minimal bulk
7. **Eco-Friendly Cases** - Sustainable materials

**For Each Product, Provide:**
- Brand name
- Product name (exact, from official listing)
- Price (USD, from official/Amazon listing)
- Material (e.g., "TPU + PC", "Silicone", "Leather")
- Key features (magnetic compatibility, kickstand, drop rating, etc.)
- Purchase links (official store, Amazon, Best Buy, etc.)
- Availability status (in stock / pre-order / out of stock)

**Important Notes:**
- Focus on cases SPECIFICALLY for Pixel 10 (not generic or other models)
- Many cases work across "Pixel 10 / 10 Pro / 10 Pro XL" - note compatibility
- Verify products are actually available for purchase
- Include both well-known brands AND lesser-known quality options
- For magnetic cases, note if they work with Pixelsnap accessories
- Include drop protection ratings when available (MIL-STD-810G, etc.)

**Output Format:**
Provide results as a markdown table or structured list that can be easily converted to JSON.
