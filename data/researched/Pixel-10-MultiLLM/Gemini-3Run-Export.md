* **UAG Pathfinder Clear ($59.95):** Offers an 18ft drop rating. It is "Made for Google" certified, ensuring the internal magnets align perfectly with the Qi2 charging coil.
* **SUPCASE Unicorn Beetle Pro ($31.99):** Includes a built-in screen protector and a detachable belt clip. Note that its magnetic strength is optimized for mounts rather than high-speed charging.

#### 3. Premium & Minimalist Cases
* **Thinborne 600D Aramid Fiber ($69.99):** The thinnest magnetic case available (0.04 inches). It feels like a "naked" phone but provides scratch and light drop protection using aerospace materials.
* **Mous Limitless 6.0 ($69.99):** Known for its "AiroShock" technology, this case comes in premium finishes like Walnut, Bamboo, and Black Leather.

#### 4. Everyday / Clear Cases
* **Spigen Ultra Hybrid Metal Ring ($69.99):** A high-end clear case that features a reinforced metal ring for superior magnetic attachment to car mounts and chargers.
* **Caseology Nano Pop ($29.99):** A budget-friendly alternative to the official Google case with a silicone feel and a distinct pop of color around the camera bar.

### Critical Compatibility Notes
* **Pixelsnap/Qi2:** All cases listed as "Yes" in the magnetic column are fully compatible with the Pixel 10's internal magnets. They will work with MagSafe-style chargers, wallets, and car mounts.
* **Wallet Cases:** Be aware that **Torro** and **CaseMe** leather wallets often lack strong internal magnets to allow for the thickness of cards; while they support wireless charging, they may not "snap" to magnetic mounts securely.
* **Model Cross-Compatibility:** While some cases are marketed as "Pixel 10 / 10 Pro," verify the camera cutout. The Pixel 10 (Base) has a dual-camera system, while the 10 Pro has a triple-lens system; Pro cases will fit the base model but will have an oversized camera cutout.
