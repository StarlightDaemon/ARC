# Pixel 10 Pro XL Research - Key Findings

## Critical Specs Discovered

| Spec | Value | Accessory Impact |
|------|-------|------------------|
| **Wired Charging** | 37W via 18-21V PPS (HIGH VOLTAGE) | Need chargers with 3.3-21V PPS range |
| **Wireless** | 25W Pixelsnap, 15W standard Qi2 | Only Pixelsnap Stand hits 25W |
| **Built-in Magnets** | YES - Qi2 native | No magnetic case required! |
| **Fingerprint** | Ultrasonic | Needs LOCA glass or film |
| **Display** | Flat, Super Actua LTPO OLED | Easy to protect |

## Charger Tiers (NEW DATA)

### Tier 1: Full 37W (21V PPS Required)
| Brand | Model | PPS Range | Notes |
|-------|-------|-----------|-------|
| Google | 45W USB-C | 3.3-21V | Reference |
| Anker | Prime 67W (A2669) | 3.3-21V | ActiveShield thermal |
| UGREEN | Nexode Pro 65W | 3.3-21V | Ultra slim |
| Shargeek | Retro 67 | 3.3-20V | LED display |

### Tier 2: Sub-Optimal (~28-30W)
| Brand | Model | Issue |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Samsung | 45W (EP-T4510) | PPS capped at 11-16V |
| Anker | Nano II 45W (older) | Lacks upper voltage |

**Key Insight:** Pixel uses HIGH VOLTAGE (18-21V) unlike Samsung's HIGH CURRENT (10V/4.5A). Different charger requirements!
